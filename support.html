How do I play PrismRush?
Match colorful prisms by connecting three or more of the same color. Create combos and use power-ups to achieve higher scores!
The game is running slowly or crashing
Try closing other apps, restarting your device, or updating to the latest iOS version. Make sure you have enough storage space available.
How do I restore my purchases?
Go to Settings in the game and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
I'm not receiving ads/rewards
Check your internet connection and ensure you have the latest app version. Ad availability may vary by region.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.
