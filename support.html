How do I play PrismRush?
Run through colorful 3D environments, collect prisms, avoid obstacles, and use power-ups to achieve higher scores in this endless runner adventure!
The game is running slowly or crashing
Try closing other apps, restarting your device, or updating to the latest iOS version. Make sure you have enough storage space available.
How do I restore my purchases?
iOS: Go to Settings in the game and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.
Android: Purchases are automatically restored when signed in with the same Google account.
I'm not receiving ads/rewards
Check your internet connection and ensure you have the latest app version. Ad availability may vary by region.
How do I request a refund?
iOS: Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support.
Android: Visit Google Play Order History or contact Google Play Support.
